2. Properties of solar flares

Basic Physics

Plasma and Magnetic Field

Plasma (conductive fluid with charged protons and electrons)
→ creates magnetic field as they move
→ magnetic fields can induce currents in a moving conductive fluid, which in turn polarizes the fluid and reciprocally changes the magnetic field itself [Magnetohydrodynamics]

Alfvén’s theorem (the frozen-in flux theorem)

The Alfvén’s theorem is usually stated in a really complex mathematical form, including Maxwell equations and magnetohydrodynamic equations. Instead of getting lost in those advanced physics equations, I’d like to focus on the following illustrations which descirbe the same idea with Alfvén theorem in a sketchy but understandable way.

Charged particles cannot cross the field lines and will bend under the action of Lorentz force. As a result, the protons and electrons must spiral around the field lines inside the plasma. This determines the inner motion pattern for plasma.

From another point of view, magnetic field cannot freely move in the sea of plasma either, due to the same reason. Thus, we say magnetic lines are frozen in. This describe the property of magnetic field inside the solar plasma.

Formation of Solar Flares

The lectures went over how the magnetic field in the solar surface cause sunsplots (the left figure after paragraph). As sunspots come close together, tens of thousands of kilometers above the spots, in the solar corona, opposing field lines squeeze close together, until eventually the field snaps into a lower-energy configuration. Since the original field has higher energy, this process release some of the field energy to heat up the plasma and eject it outward. Such process may explain the CME occuring along with the solar flare.

Now we may also explain why we get all kinds of EM radiation:

  • Radio-wavelength radiation is generated by the synchrotron process
  • The materials will fall down and collide with ions in the lower atmosphere. High energy particles will also accelerated toward chromoshpere to undergo some nuclear reactions. X-rays and γ\gammaγ-rays can be generated in both processes.
  • Hα emission may be produced by recombining electrons and protons to hydrogen

  1. Among 365 G-type stars with observed superflares, 101 of them are slowly rotating stars. This implies that quite an amount of observed superflares can generated from slowly-rotating solar-type stars.
  2. Power law function, although it is invented to fit the distribution of solar flares, is still working for the distribution of superflares generated by hundreds of different stars. This may imply the similarities on mechanism between flares and superflares.

Direct Evidence

Superflare Signatures on Earth:

High energy protons can produce C-14 and Be-10 in Earth’s atmosphere. The former can be detected in tree rings; the latter in the ice caps of Greenland and Antarctica.

Result:

  1. Spikes of isotopes are found around 774CE. One study calculate the flare energy is 2×10262×10^{26}2×1026J.
  2. Frequency ~ once several thousand years & No flares around 102710^{27}1027 J.

The result showed that there was a solarflare exceeding the minimum required to become a superflare. However, the overall statistics show that such an extreme event occurs at most once in several millennia, much more rarely than the few centuries implied by Kepler, and there is no evidence of huge 102710^{27}1027 joule superflares. Stellar data on flares don’t match the statistics of solar energetic particles pretty well.
