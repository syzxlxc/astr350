Coronal Mass Ejection(CME)

Solar flares are always accompanied by coronal mass ejection(CME), which are large expulsions of plasma from the Sun’s corona.

NOTE:
CME and solar flare are different in many ways. Solar flare is mainly composed of EM radiation (thus travels in light speed) together with some high energy particles, CME emits enourmous plasma and travels at a more lersurely pace.
